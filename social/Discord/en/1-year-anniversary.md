:tada: Today we celebrate the **1-year anniversary of Forge of Games**! :tada:

What started as a website with just a few pages has grown into a **powerful hub of tools, data, and insights** for players. Plus, it’s **open-source** and available in **10 in-game languages**, making it accessible to players worldwide!

Here’s what Forge of Games offers today:
- **[City Planner](https://forgeofgames.com/city-planner)** – Build and optimize your capital and allied culture cities. Explore the **Inspirations** page for high-efficiency layouts created by other players.
- **[Battle Log](https://forgeofgames.com/battle-log)** – Track real battles submitted by the community, with powerful filters. The **Battle Timeline** shows squad placements, equipment, and relic usage.
- **[Heroes Database](https://forgeofgames.com/heroes)** – Discover all heroes, with dedicated pages displaying stats, progression calculators, and insights into Equipment, Relics, and Battle performance.
- **Command Center** – Use **Profiles** to organize your battle teams, **Equipment** to view all your own gear, and **My Battles** to track battles you’ve submitted to the system.
- **[Stats Hub](https://forgeofgames.com/stats-hub)** – Explore leaderboards, metrics, and trend analysis for players, alliances, and heroes.
- **Tools** – Includes the **Research Calculator**, **World Wonder Calculator**, and **Building Cost Calculator** (also integrated into City Planner).
- **Campaign and Treasure Hunt** – Find all encounters, with detailed info on adversary units and rewards.
- **Buildings and Wonders** – Quick access to construction, upgrade, and progression costs.
- **[Browser extension](https://forgeofgames.com/help/browser-extension)** – Share in-game data and export your cities, equipment, and hero roster.
- **[Alliance Manager](https://discord.com/channels/1199677867341140049/1433919108751167610)** – Keep track of your alliance’s internal treasure hunt rankings.
